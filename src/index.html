How to Use

1
Open iCloud

Click "Open iCloud" to open a window to iCloud's Data Recovery page.

2
Sign In

In the browser window, sign in with your Apple ID and complete 2FA. Navigate to the "Restore Files" section.

3
Wait for Items to Load

Let the file list start loading. You don't need to wait for all items -- a few hundred is enough to start.

4
Scan Page

Click "Scan Page" to detect selectable items. The counters will update with what was found.

5
Select 500

Click "Select 500" to automatically check the next 500 unchecked items in the list.

6
Click Restore

Click "Click Restore" to find and press the Restore button on the page (even if it's scrolled off-screen).

7
Repeat

Wait for the restore to process, then repeat steps 5-6 for the next batch. Keep going until all items are restored.

Tip: If buttons aren't working, try scanning the page first to detect the latest page structure.
